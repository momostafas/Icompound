Close to the charm of DAMAC Hills and yet hidden away from the bustle is DAMAC Lagoons – a vibrant master community in Dubai, inspired by iconic Mediterranean destinations. At DAMAC Lagoons, the most elegant villas and townhouses are surrounded by azure lagoons, white sandy beaches, tropical island vibes and other enchanting experiences, across over 45 million square feet.
